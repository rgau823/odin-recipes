Lasagna Recipe

"I never met a lasagna I didn't like..." a truly timeless line because it's true. I love this layered meat pasta dish that borders the line between casserole and cake. I take some shortcuts with this recipe, be warned if you're a stickler for the authentic thing.

Ingredients

  • 1 box lasagna sheets
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1 package italian sausage (usually about 3/4 lbs)
  • 400g or small container of ricotta cheese
  • 300g shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare lasagna sheets according to package directions
  2. Dice onion and add to lightly oiled pan. Cook until translucent
  3. Squeeze filling out of sausages into pan. Cook filling until done
  4. Add tomato sauce to pan. Once sauce bubbling, turn off heat.
  5. Assemble lasagna in baking pan in following order. Layer of sauce on bottom, then add one layer of lasagna. Then ricotta and mozzarella. Then add another sheet of lasagna and then sauce.
  6. Repeat the above step until you run out of ingredients. You will likely have about 4-6 layers.
  7. On the top of layer, add your remaining mozzarella and sauce.
  8. Bake in oven at 350F for 30 minutes
  9. Let lasagna cool down for 10 minutes before cutting into squares
